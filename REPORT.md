# EU4 mission-tree matchup analysis (all DLC enabled)

Generated by parsing your installed game files (`missions/*.txt`, `map/{area,region,superregion}.txt`).

**Definitions**
- **Missions** = total missions across every *active, tag-locked* mission series that tag can see. Legacy pre-DLC trees (hidden because you own the newer DLC) are excluded.
- **F** = formable nation (you must form it in-game before its mission tree unlocks — a time/difficulty cost, not a 1444 start).
- **Theater** = dominant superregion by mission focus.
- **Core regions** = regions targeted by enough missions to be a real objective (weight ≥ max(3, 12% of the nation's missions)).
- A **clean matchup** requires: (1) zero shared *core* regions, (2) no shared mission series (not the same lineage/formable chain), (3) similar size (smaller/larger ≥ 0.72 **or** within 12 missions).
- **Size is objective (mission count). Difficulty is NOT encoded in the files** — treat the count as the race-length proxy and judge difficulty separately.

> Parser caveats: a few nations whose trees use unusual `potential` patterns (e.g. Mughals, Ming) are under-counted here; numbers reflect tag-locked series only, not generic/shared trees a nation can also pursue.
